Help

Hello and welcome to The Fisher! Your goal in this game is to travel around Europe and catch every fish species in the available countries. Starting off you will have some fishing gear and a bit of money in your backpack, so make sure to check that out in the "Inventory" menu before going fishing. The shop is accessible everywhere so if your line breaks, or you need new lures, just head to the shop and buy new equipment with the money you have. You can earn more money by catching fish. In the "Map" menu you can choose where you want to travel to by clicking a button with the countries name. The "Progress" menu keeps track of all the fish you have and haven't caught. So if you are wondering what fish species you are still missing, the "Progress" menu is a great place to find that out.
